Mormon Standard Works vs. 19th Century Lit

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (colloquially referred to as Mormons) believe that an American named Joseph Smith translated their holy work of scripture, The Book of Mormon, by the gift and power of God. The LDS Church claims that the book is a translation of an ancient American text. This is an experiment to observe the LDS Church's holy works and how they are similar to other texts that were contemporary to Jospeh Smith.
